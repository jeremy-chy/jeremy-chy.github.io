Hi there, I'm Hanyang(Jeremy) Chen

I am a second-year MSCS student in UIUC, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Tong Zhang. I also work closely with two other amazing advisors, Prof. Manling Li and Prof. Huan Zhang. Prior to this, I obtained my Bachelor of Science in HKU, with the thesis advised by Prof. Yanchao Yang and Prof. Yi Ma.

My interest generally lies in Learning Algorithms for Decision Making, currently I work on problems related to VLM Post-training, with application on Embodied AI and Robotics.
